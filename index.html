Who We Are

Tahoe Property Watch LLC is a local Lake Tahoe family run business that helps short term rental (STR) owners manage their property and stay in legal compliance.

We describe ourselves as "property watchers" & not "property managers" because we allow the owner to keep control of operating their rental while having a trusted local helping hand.

We like to describe our services like having a really friendly neighbor that lives next door to your rental. You still run the rental on your own but when you need a simple task done we are always here to help.
